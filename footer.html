About SUMM

The Seminary on Undergraduate Mathematics in Montreal (SUMM), is organized by undergraduate students from Montreal universities. The main objective is to create an environment facilitating exchange of ideas and interests as well as allowing students to network.

SUMM is for undergraduate student in mathematics or related domains. This year, the conference will be held on January 8 and 9, online.
